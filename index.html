PLAY THE GAME AT FULL COMPUTER WINDOW SIZE AND TURN VOLUME UP!

Welcome to the Super Mario Collector Game! You will be fighting Bowser for your beloved Princess Peach. The only way to win against Bowser is to match your total score to Bowser's castle chamber code. But beware! If you fail to match the chamber code, Princess Peach will be locked away forever.

Each of the power-ups below will have a random number associated with it.

You're goal is to add up the total random numbers to the chamber code ultimately unlocking Princess Peach from her prison.
